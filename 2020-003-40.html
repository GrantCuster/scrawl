108 words Rough music purchasing plan: I've been meaning to switch off Spotify to bandcamp, but unsure about how to start the transition. So I'm planning a system. I keep listening to Spotify, tracking what I listen to using last.fm. At the end of each month I buy three albums. Which albums I buy is based on a combination of: what I listen to the most + bias toward active artists (release in the past two years?) + bias towards less popular artists (probably using a threshold of global plays on spotify or last.fm) + available to buy on bandcamp. Maybe I'll make a script to calculate this for myself.
